title: The Six Engines
summary: Outbound, inbound, content, revenue, agents, and the work OS
---

AGNB is organized into six engines. Each is a self-contained growth function the agents operate.

## Autonomous Agents
Agents that plan, code, ship, and run growth ops. Give them a goal; they do the reps — campaigns, content, tickets, syncs. Approval gates when you want them.

## Outbound
Campaigns ranked by reply rate, multi-sender across email + LinkedIn, AI reply drafts, and sequence control. Stop guessing which campaign works — every one is ranked.

## Inbound
Every mention, demo, and deal — captured, attributed, and scored. Share-of-voice across LLMs, pipeline + funnel sync, and review monitoring.

## Content Studio
Idea → published, on rails. Mine trends, auto-draft blog and LinkedIn, schedule, then track GSC rank and content gaps. The flywheel runs itself.

## Revenue
Attribution that isn't a guess. Forecast, renewals, invoicing, and win/loss — so you know what's driving revenue and what's about to churn before it does.

## Work OS
Issues, routines, goals, and approvals — the orchestration layer that schedules, assigns, and drives every engine above.
