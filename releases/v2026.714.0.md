# Paperclip v2026.714.0

## Improvements

- **Open-by-default company skill workflows** - Same-company actors can install, import, create, edit, test, update, reset, and remove company skills without first receiving a `skills:create` grant or enabling `canCreateSkills`. Companies that need tighter governance can configure explicit company skill policy restrictions, while core company-boundary, source-containment, validation, redaction, and activity-log protections remain enforced. ([#9564](https://github.com/paperclipai/paperclip/pull/9564), @cryppadotta)

## Upgrade Guidance

- Companies with no explicit company skill policy become open by default after upgrading. Existing explicit restrictions continue to be enforced by core, including when Paperclip EE is unavailable.
- Legacy `skills:create` grants and `canCreateSkills` settings no longer act as prerequisites for ordinary skill work. They may remain stored for compatibility, but operators should not rely on a missing grant or disabled legacy flag as a denial.
- Before upgrading, operators who previously treated missing grants as a restriction should configure an explicit company skill policy that denies the required actions or switches the company to a default-deny preset with explicit allow rules.
