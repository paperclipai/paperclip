# Prompt Patterns

## Social Image

```text
Create a square social post image for bankruptcy attorneys.
Goal: make missed intake calls feel expensive and urgent.
Scene: a clean law firm intake desk after hours, phone activity visible, no
people, no readable private client data.
Style: premium legal technology brand, confident, clean, credible, no hype.
Brand: True Blue #003366, Soft Linen #F5F0E8, white, charcoal.
Copy/text: "Every missed call has a case value."
Avoid: vendor names, fake results, red/purple palette, court seals, stock-photo
cliches, robots, watermarks.
Output: 1:1 image.
```

## Campaign Banner

```text
Create a wide campaign banner for a law-firm AI operating system.
Goal: show 24/7 intake coverage without sounding generic.
Scene: modern intake command center with call, document, and lead pipeline
signals represented abstractly on clean screens.
Style: premium B2B legal tech, quiet confidence, realistic lighting.
Copy/text: no text.
Avoid: vendor names, logos, testimonials, fake metrics, red/purple palette,
watermarks.
Output: 16:9 image.
```

## Text-To-Video

```text
Create an 8-second cinematic product explainer clip for bankruptcy attorneys.
Shot 1: after-hours law firm office, incoming call lights up.
Shot 2: intake workflow activates across clean dashboard screens.
Shot 3: completed lead card moves into attorney review.
Motion: slow, precise camera movement; no chaotic UI animation.
Style: premium legal technology brand, credible, restrained, no hype.
Text: no on-screen text.
Avoid: vendor names, fake law firm names, fake results, court seals, robots,
watermarks, distorted hands or faces.
Output: 16:9 video.
```

## Image-To-Video

```text
Animate the supplied campaign image into a 6-second social clip.
Motion: subtle parallax, gentle screen glow, slow push-in.
Keep all text legible and unchanged.
Do not add new claims, vendor names, people, logos, or watermarks.
Output: 9:16 vertical video.
```
