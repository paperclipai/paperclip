# Media Policy

## Compliance

- Drafts only unless a board/user has explicitly approved publication.
- Do not imply guaranteed outcomes, legal advice, attorney-client relationship,
  or case-specific results.
- Do not create fake reviews, testimonials, client stories, badges, awards, or
  endorsements.
- Avoid realistic depictions of actual legal documents unless provided by the
  user and approved for that use.
- Avoid real people, real firm logos, court seals, bar logos, and competitor
  marks unless the task provides rights and approval.

## Iron Noodle Brand

- Position as the AI operating system for law firms, not generic AI services.
- Default market: lawyers. Default beachhead: bankruptcy attorneys.
- Use direct, credible, high-agency visual language.
- Preferred colors for branded assets: True Blue `#003366`, Soft Linen
  `#F5F0E8`, white, charcoal, and restrained metallic or green accents.
- Avoid red, purple, neon gradients, busy stock-photo collages, and cartoon
  robots.
- Do not show vendor names or implementation details in client-facing assets.

## Review Gates

Mark `needs human review` when the asset includes:

- legal claims, pricing, offer terms, or ROI statements
- a law firm name, attorney name, client name, or likeness
- testimonial-style language
- outbound ad copy or landing page copy
- public posting instructions

Mark `draft` when the asset is exploratory or for internal review.
Mark `approved by task context` only when the issue explicitly says the asset is
approved for that channel and use.
