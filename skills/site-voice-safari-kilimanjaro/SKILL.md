# Site Voice — Safari Kilimanjaro

## Brand in one line

> The Kilimanjaro + Tanzania safari combo, done right. WhatsApp us; we'll put the whole trip together without hidden fees.

## Who we are

- **Combo specialist** — Kilimanjaro climb + Tanzania safari as one package. Not two vendors stapled together.
- **Blue ocean** — no direct competitor owns this combo positioning. We lean hard into it.
- **WhatsApp-first** — conversion funnel is WhatsApp inquiry, not form. CTA placement is aggressive.
- **300+ pages, 10+ locales** — big library.
- **No hidden fees** — published pricing for combos includes permits, park fees, domestic flights.

## Voice anchors

| Trait | Yes | No |
|---|---|---|
| Tone | Energetic, confident, action-forward | Aspirational, vague, passive |
| Posture | "Let's do it. Here's how." | "Dream it. We'll make it happen." |
| Rhythm | Short sentences. Direct verbs. Concrete specifics. | Long paragraphs. Abstract "experience" language. |
| Energy | Adventure + reliability | Hype or over-promise |
| Honesty | "Yes, summit day is brutal. Here's how we prepare you." | Glossing over difficulty |

## Vocabulary

### Use
- "Combo", "combined", "together", "one trip" — emphasize the unique positioning
- "Summit", "acclimatization", "Marangu/Machame/Lemosho route" — Kili-savvy
- "Serengeti", "Ngorongoro" — safari-savvy
- "Private", "direct", "WhatsApp us", "no broker", "no hidden fees"
- "7+3 = 10 days", "8 + 4 = 12 days" — explicit combo math
- "Summit success rate" (our data is public: % by route)
- Emoji (sparingly, on social/CTAs only, not in body): 🏔 🦁 🐘 (no more than 1 per page element)

### Avoid
- "Epic", "awesome", "amazing" — low-signal
- "Adventure of a lifetime" — cliché
- Corporate safety-theater ("our safety standards are the highest") — everyone claims this
- "Bucket list" — we're beyond this
- "Book now" — WhatsApp us > book now, for our funnel
- Passive voice ("the mountain will be climbed" → "you'll climb the mountain")

### Forbidden (hook v12 P9)
- Micato, Altezza, Nomad Tanzania, Tanzania Specialist, Bobby Camping, Karibu Kilifair, Singita, Asilia, Four Seasons, andBeyond — never named.

## Sentence patterns

### Openings
- "Seven days on Kilimanjaro. Three days in the Serengeti. One trip."
- "We put the Kili climb FIRST in the itinerary. Here's why."
- "90% of our clients summit on their first attempt. Here's what we do differently."

### Transitions
- "Here's the honest part:"
- "What most operators won't tell you:"
- "Here's what happens on day 5:"

### Closings (CTA)
- "WhatsApp us" (primary CTA everywhere)
- "Talk to Don on WhatsApp"
- "Message us for a combo quote" (secondary)

## Paragraph feel (example)

### ✅ On-brand
> Summit day starts at 11 pm. You'll have been on the mountain for 5 days. You're tired. You're cold. We give you hot ginger tea, two layers of down, a guide who climbs Kili 40 times a year. You start walking. Seven hours later, at 7 am, you're at Uhuru Peak — 5,895 metres. We take your photo. You head down. That afternoon you're sleeping hard at Mweka Camp. In 36 hours you'll be in a Land Cruiser watching lions in the Serengeti. This is what the combo trip is: the hardest thing you've ever done, followed by the most beautiful.

### ❌ Off-brand
> Embark on the adventure of a lifetime with our exclusive Kilimanjaro climbing and Tanzania safari experience! Our expert guides will help you conquer the roof of Africa and then enjoy breathtaking wildlife viewing in the legendary Serengeti. Contact us today to start planning your dream African adventure!

## OK to mention / Don't mention

| OK | Don't |
|---|---|
| "Summit success rate: 90% on 8-day Lemosho" | "Highest success rate in the industry" |
| "From $6,400 for the 10-day combo" | "Best value combos" (vague) |
| "Permit fees included: $2,000 Kili + $400 safari parks" | "All-inclusive" (vague) |
| "WhatsApp: 9am–10pm EAT, replies within 1 hour" | "24/7 support" (we're not a call center) |
| "Route: Lemosho 8-day. Why not Marangu? We'll explain." | Glossing over route choice |

## WhatsApp CTA — hard rules

- **Every landing page**: WhatsApp CTA above the fold.
- **Every itinerary page**: sticky WhatsApp button.
- **Every blog post**: WhatsApp CTA in conclusion.
- **Every FAQ**: "Still have questions? WhatsApp us."
- **Mobile nav**: dedicated WhatsApp link (green, unmissable).

## Locale notes

- 10+ locales. Voice travels well. Adjust for WhatsApp adoption:
  - Not universal in US, JP, KR (lower usage) → offer email fallback in those locales.
  - Universal in IT, DE, ES, NL, PT, HI — lean into WhatsApp.
- Preserve energy in translation. German "WhatsApp uns" works. Japanese needs softer phrasing ("WhatsAppでご連絡ください").
