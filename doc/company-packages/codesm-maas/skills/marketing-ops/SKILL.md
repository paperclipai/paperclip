---
name: marketing-ops
description: Plan, prioritize, and improve marketing operations work across growth, fulfillment, and reporting.
---

Use this skill to turn marketing service work into a practical operating plan.

When applying it:

- Identify the client outcome and business context.
- Separate fulfillment, reporting, QA, and approval work.
- Convert vague requests into specific tasks with owners and next actions.
- Surface assumptions, missing data, and approval needs.
- Keep recommendations practical for a multi-client queue.
