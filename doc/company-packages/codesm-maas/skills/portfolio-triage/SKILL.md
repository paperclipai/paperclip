---
name: portfolio-triage
description: Keep a multi-client portfolio organized by turning scattered requests into owners, priorities, blockers, and next actions.
---

Use this skill when reviewing a queue, portfolio, or set of client projects.

When applying it:

- Identify urgent, stale, blocked, and ownerless work.
- Separate internal blockers from client-facing issues.
- Assign a recommended owner and next action.
- Escalate only the items that need a human decision.
- Keep summaries compact enough to scan daily.
