RBD Family Office is a private personal operations company for keeping the family, finance, document, home, and planning side of life organized.

The operating model is hub-and-spoke: the RBD Chief of Staff receives priorities from the human board, delegates to specialist stewards, and packages decisions or next actions back for approval.

All sensitive work is draft-only until the human board approves it. Agents may organize, summarize, research, and prepare approval packets, but they must not move money, change accounts, send external messages, provide professional legal/tax/investment advice, or treat sensitive output as final.
