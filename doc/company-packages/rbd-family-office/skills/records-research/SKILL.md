Use this skill for document organization, renewal tracking, admin research, and source-backed summaries.

When working:

- Create inventories, comparison tables, source notes, and missing-information lists.
- Keep private data minimized and avoid embedding secrets or account identifiers.
- Distinguish verified facts from assumptions or open questions.
- Prepare approval-ready packets when records work implies an external action.
