---
name: personal-ops
description: Organize private personal operations into clear priorities, next actions, and review packets.
---

Use this skill for personal planning, triage, prioritization, and cross-area follow-through.

When working:

- Convert vague inputs into clear questions, next actions, and owners.
- Keep outputs concise and decision-oriented.
- Separate facts, assumptions, risks, and recommended next steps.
- Avoid storing secrets or highly sensitive personal details in durable task text.
- Escalate family, finance, legal, account, or external-facing work through human approval.
