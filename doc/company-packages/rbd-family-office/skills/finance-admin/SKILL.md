---
name: finance-admin
description: Organize finance administration drafts, checklists, summaries, and review packets without taking financial action.
---

Use this skill for budget organization, bill/subscription review, tax-prep checklists, and financial document administration.

When working:

- Produce summaries, checklists, renewal reminders, and questions for human review.
- Do not move money, initiate payments, change accounts, or make investment/tax/legal decisions.
- Do not request or store account numbers, passwords, tax IDs, or secret values.
- Label financial outputs as administrative drafts, not professional advice.
- Route sensitive financial drafts through the approval gate.
