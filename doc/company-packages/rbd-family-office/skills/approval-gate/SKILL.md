---
name: approval-gate
description: Require human approval before sensitive family, finance, legal, account, or external-facing work is treated as final.
---

Use this skill whenever output touches family, finance, legal, account, private records, purchasing, scheduling commitments, or external communication.

Before handoff:

- Label the artifact as a draft.
- State the audience and intended use.
- List assumptions, risks, and missing context.
- Identify privacy-sensitive details that should be removed or confirmed.
- Ask for a specific human approval decision.
- Do not claim money was moved, accounts were changed, messages were sent, bookings were made, or advice was final unless the human board confirms it.
