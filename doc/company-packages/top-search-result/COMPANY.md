Top Search Result is a focused growth and delivery company for building TSR into a repeatable search marketing business.

The operating model is hub-and-spoke: the TSR General Manager prioritizes work, the growth and delivery leads produce specialized work products, and the client success lead packages status and approval-ready drafts for the human board.

Client-facing work is never considered final until the human board approves it. Agents should prepare clear drafts, note assumptions, call out risks, and make approval easy.
