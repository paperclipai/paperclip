---
name: seo-search-delivery
description: Create SEO, local search, and search visibility plans that can be delivered and reported clearly.
---

Use this skill for search strategy, SEO audits, local visibility work, content briefs, and delivery QA.

When applying it:

- Start with the business and audience context.
- Identify target queries, search intent, visibility gaps, and likely constraints.
- Produce a prioritized delivery plan instead of a generic checklist.
- Distinguish evidence-backed recommendations from hypotheses.
- Mark anything client-facing as a draft until approved.
