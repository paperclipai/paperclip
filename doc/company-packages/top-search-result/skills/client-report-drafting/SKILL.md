---
name: client-report-drafting
description: Draft clear client status reports and approval packets for marketing operations work.
---

Use this skill to turn work progress into client-ready reporting drafts.

When applying it:

- Summarize what happened, why it matters, and what happens next.
- Separate internal risk notes from client-facing language.
- Include assumptions, missing data, and approval questions.
- Keep the tone direct, professional, and easy for a human to edit.
- Never treat a draft as approved or sent.
