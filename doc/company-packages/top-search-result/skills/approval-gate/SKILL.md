---
name: approval-gate
description: Require human approval before using client-facing or prospect-facing output.
---

Use this skill whenever output may leave the internal company context.

Before handoff:

- Label the artifact as a draft.
- State the audience and intended use.
- List assumptions, risks, and any missing context.
- Ask for a specific approval decision.
- Do not claim the artifact has been sent, published, or accepted unless the human board confirms it.
