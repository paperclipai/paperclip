---
name: marketing-ops
description: Plan, prioritize, and improve marketing operations work across growth, fulfillment, and reporting.
---

Use this skill to turn broad marketing goals into a practical operating plan.

When applying it:

- Identify the intended business outcome.
- Separate acquisition, fulfillment, reporting, and operational cleanup work.
- Convert vague goals into specific tasks with owners and next actions.
- Surface assumptions and approval needs.
- Keep recommendations concise enough for a human operator to approve or redirect.
