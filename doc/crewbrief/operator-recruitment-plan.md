# Operator Recruitment Plan — CrewBrief Beta

## Goal

Recruit 10–20 operators from target segments for the private beta (Weeks 1–4).

---

## Target Segments

### Primary: Part 135 Charter / Fractional Operators
- Small-to-mid-size charter operators (5–50 aircraft)
- Corporate flight departments
- Fractional providers
- **Why**: High briefing volume, multiple crew per aircraft, fragmented workflows
- **Examples**: boutique charter firms, regional air taxi operators, corporate flight departments at F500 companies

### Secondary: Part 91 Operators
- High-net-worth private flight departments
- Owner-operators flying complex itineraries
- **Why**: Value premium experience, willing to pay for quality

### Tertiary: Cargo / Regional Part 121
- Small cargo operators
- Regional airlines
- **Why**: High volume, efficiency-driven

---

## Phase 1: Direct Outreach (Weeks 1–2)

### Channel: LinkedIn
- **Target titles**: Director of Operations, Chief Pilot, Fleet Manager, Head of Aviation, Aviation Manager
- **Target companies**: Part 135 operators with 5+ aircraft
- **Method**: Personalized connection requests + InMail
- **Message template**:

```
Hi [Name],

I'm launching CrewBrief — a briefing automation tool for aviation operators.
We automatically compile weather, NOTAMs, route data, fuel, and risk
assessment into one polished briefing, delivered to every crew member
before duty.

We're recruiting 10–20 beta testers. No cost during beta, no commitment —
just honest feedback from people who live this every day.

After beta, CrewBrief will remain a low-cost software product — $9.99
one-time or $4.99/month (beta testers lock in the lowest rate forever).

Would you be open to a 5-min chat or a beta trial?

Best,
Grace
CrewBrief Operations
```

### Channel: Email
- Source contacts from: aviation industry directories, conference attendee lists (NBAA, etc.)
- **Subject**: "Cut briefing time in half — beta invitation"
- Same messaging as LinkedIn

### Channel: Aviation Forums
- **Subreddits**: r/flying, r/aviation, r/aviationmaintenance
- **PPRuNe**: Operations & Safety forum
- **JetCareers**: Crew forum
- **AOPA Forums**: Pilot-to-pilot
- **Format**: Not spam — participate genuinely, offer free trial in signature or relevant threads

---

## Phase 2: Partner-Led Recruitment (Weeks 2–4)

### FBO Partnerships
- Partner with 2–3 FBOs at major GA airports
- FBO recommends CrewBrief to based operators
- CrewBrief provides co-branded flyers for FBO counters

### Industry Influencers
- Aviation YouTubers / podcasters: offer sponsored episodes or guest spots
- Aviation safety consultants: offer white-label briefings

### Scheduling System Integrators
- Partner with flight scheduling software providers to offer CrewBrief as an add-on
- Target: JetInsight, Sabre, FL3XX, AeroPal

---

## Phase 3: Warm Referrals (Ongoing)

Every beta tester is asked:
- "Know another operator who'd benefit?"
- Referral link + "you both get priority support"
- Monthly referral leaderboard
