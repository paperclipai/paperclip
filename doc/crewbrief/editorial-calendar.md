|---------|-----------|-------------------|
| Weather Briefing | aviation weather automation | "automated METAR briefing", "integrated weather NOTAM briefing", "pilot weather data consolidation" |

### On-Page SEO Requirements

All articles include:
- Meta description (140-160 chars) with primary keyword
- URL slug derived from title
- H2/H3 structure with keyword-rich headings
- Internal links to related CrewBrief articles (2-3 per post)
- CTA to `https://crewbrief.avva.aero` with relevant anchor text
- Open Graph image placeholder

### Tracking (via PostHog)

- Page view events per article slug
- Scroll depth (25%, 50%, 75%, 100%)
- CTA click events
- Time on page
- Internal link clicks (cross-article navigation)
- Source/medium attribution
