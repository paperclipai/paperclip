## Getting Started

### What is CrewBrief?

CrewBrief delivers premium aviation briefings — weather, NOTAMs, route data, and crew notices — customized for your flights. Receive them automatically before departure via push notification, email, or SMS.

### System Requirements

- **iOS:** 15.0+
- **Android:** 12+
- **Web:** Chrome, Safari, Firefox (latest 2 versions)
- **Internet connection required** for initial delivery; briefings cached for offline viewing

### Account Setup

1. Download the CrewBrief app or visit crewbrief.avva.aero
2. Tap "Sign Up" and enter your email
3. Verify your email address
4. Complete your profile (airline, aircraft type, home base)
5. Set notification preferences

## Briefings

### What's in a Briefing?

| Section | Description |
|---|---|
| Weather | METAR, TAF for departure, destination, alternates |
| NOTAMs | Relevant notices for route and airports |
| Route | Waypoints, altitude, estimated time |
| Fuel | Fuel calculations, reserves (detailed briefings) |
| Crew Notices | Company-specific announcements |
| Risk Assessment | Executive summary of key concerns |

### Briefing Formats

| Format | Read Time | Contents |
|---|---|---|
| Standard | 2-3 min | Essential weather + NOTAMs |
| Detailed | 5-7 min | Full METAR/TAF, winds aloft, alternates, fuel |
| Executive | 1 page | Risk summary + key highlights |

### How to Read a Briefing

1. Open the CrewBrief app
2. Tap "Briefings" tab
3. Select the flight from the list
4. Swipe through sections or tap to expand
5. Tap "Acknowledge" to confirm receipt

## Schedule

### Setting Up Your Schedule

1. Go to "Schedule" tab
2. Tap "Add Flight" or import from roster
3. Enter: flight number, date/time, departure, destination
4. Select briefing format (Standard / Detailed / Executive)
5. Set delivery time (default: 2 hours before departure)
6. Confirm

### Editing or Cancelling

- Tap the flight in Schedule
- Select "Edit" or "Cancel"
- Changes apply to upcoming delivery only

### Auto-Schedule

Connect your airline roster to automatically populate your schedule. Supported formats: CSV, API integration (contact support to set up).

## Account & Profile

### Profile Settings

- **Airline & Aircraft** — used to customize briefing content
- **Home Base** — default airport
- **Languages** — briefing language preference
- **Notifications** — push, email, SMS toggles and timing

| Channel | Default | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| Push | On | Time-sensitive delivery alerts |
| Email | On | Full briefing text |

## Troubleshooting

### Briefing Not Delivered

1. Check notification settings are enabled
2. Ensure app has background refresh permission
3. Verify flight is still in your schedule
4. Force close and reopen the app
5. Contact support if persists

### App Won't Open / Crashes

1. Restart device
2. Update to latest app version
3. Reinstall the app (data is stored server-side)
4. Contact support

### Wrong Briefing Content

1. Verify aircraft type is correct in Profile
2. Check route and airports in Schedule
3. Contact support with flight details

### Can't Log In

1. Tap "Forgot Password" on login screen
2. Check email for reset link
3. Ensure email is the one used during signup
4. Contact support for account lookup

## FAQ

### General

**Q: Is CrewBrief free?**
A: CrewBrief is currently in beta — join the waitlist at crewbrief.avva.aero for free access. Post-launch, CrewBrief is available as a $9.99 one-time purchase or $4.99/month subscription. See [Pricing](#pricing) for details.

**Q: Can I use CrewBrief internationally?**
A: Yes — coverage includes most major airports worldwide.

**Q: What languages are supported?**
A: English, with additional languages planned.

**Q: Can I share a briefing with my First Officer?**
A: Yes — use the Share button within any briefing.

### Technical

**Q: How much data does a briefing use?**
A: Standard briefing ≈ 200KB; Detailed ≈ 500KB; Executive ≈ 100KB.

**Q: Can I use CrewBrief offline?**
A: Briefings are cached on delivery. You can view delivered briefings offline up to 30 days.

**Q: Which devices are supported?**
A: iPhone (iOS 15+), Android (12+), and web browser.

**Q: How do I update my email address?**
A: Profile → Account → Email → Verify new address.

### Billing

**Q: How do I switch between one-time and subscription?**
A: Contact support@crewbrief.com to switch between one-time purchase and subscription billing. See [Pricing](#pricing) for plan details.

**Q: Can I cancel anytime?**
A: Yes — cancel from Profile → Subscription. Access continues until end of billing period.

**Q: Do you offer team/airline pricing?**
A: Yes — contact support@crewbrief.com for custom team and airline pricing.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: Credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex). Enterprise customers can request invoice billing.

**Q: Is there an annual discount?**
A: Not currently — CrewBrief is priced at $9.99 one-time or $4.99/month. Beta testers lock in the lowest rate we'll ever offer.
