AJ AI Services Pvt Ltd is an AI-powered company on a mission to build the #1 social media handling app targeting $1M MRR. The company uses a hub-and-spoke org model where the CEO (AJ) delegates to department leads who each manage their own specialist sub-teams.

The core product is a **LinkedIn post generation engine** that takes topics and prompts and outputs engaging, brand-consistent content — with a target of shipping MVP v1 within 24 hours.

## Workflow

Work flows from CEO → department leads → specialists:

1. **CEO (AJ)** receives goals and priorities from the board, delegates to department leads
2. **CTO (TechLead)** owns the engineering roadmap; delegates CI/CD and security tasks to DevOpsEngine and SecureGuard
3. **Social Media Manager (SocialSage)** owns the content pipeline; coordinates IdeaSpark (ideation), DesignPro (design), and PublishBot (publishing)
4. **Program Manager (ProjectPilot)** tracks cross-team dependencies and maintains delivery cadence
5. **Responsible AI Officer (EthicsWatch)** reviews all AI-generated content against safety guidelines
6. **Event Manager (EventMaster)** plans launches, webinars, and community activations aligned with the content strategy
