Run the weekly content performance review and deliver the analytics digest to the content team.

## Checklist

1. Pull performance data for all posts published in the past 7 days
2. Calculate engagement rates and compare to 30-day baseline
3. Identify the top 3 and bottom 3 performing posts with analysis of why
4. Check audience growth rate: new followers this week vs prior 4-week average
5. Review any active A/B tests: update status and preliminary results
6. Draft weekly performance digest: top performers, trend analysis, recommendations for next week's content
7. Post digest as task comment and @-mention SocialSage and IdeaSpark
8. Flag any anomalies (viral posts or unusually low engagement) to SocialSage immediately
