## Scope

Design and deliver the executive risk posture dashboard that synthesises signals from all security functions into a single composite risk score with trend analysis and board-ready reporting.

## Deliverables

1. Composite risk scoring model (weighted inputs from threat, vuln, incident, compliance)
2. Risk dashboard: composite score, trend chart, top-5 risk drivers, heat map
3. Automated weekly risk posture report delivered to ShieldCEO
4. Alert system: CEO notified immediately when composite score drops below 60
5. Monthly risk register with treatment status for all identified risks
6. Board-level risk narrative template (non-technical language, likelihood × impact framing)

## Success Criteria

- Risk scoring model approved by ShieldCEO
- Dashboard live with data from all four input domains
- First weekly report delivered and accepted by CEO
- Alert mechanism tested and verified (trigger, notification, acknowledgment flow)
- Risk register populated with ≥ 10 identified risks and treatment plans
