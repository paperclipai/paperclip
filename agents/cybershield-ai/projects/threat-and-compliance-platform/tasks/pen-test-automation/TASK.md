## Scope

Design and deliver the monthly penetration test programme. Establish scope templates, tooling, and reporting standards. Execute the first engagement and deliver a full findings report.

## Deliverables

1. Engagement scope template with rules of engagement checklist
2. Methodology document: PTES + OWASP Testing Guide adapted for CyberShield AI clients
3. Tooling configuration: Metasploit, Burp Suite, Nmap, Nikto integration
4. First live pen test engagement: web app + API + network infrastructure
5. First full findings report with CVSS scores and remediation guidance
6. Re-test workflow: how closure of findings is validated within 30 days

## Success Criteria

- Scope template and methodology approved by ShieldCEO before first engagement
- First engagement completed with zero rules-of-engagement violations
- Full findings report delivered to CISO within 5 business days of engagement close
- All Critical/High findings tracked in VulnBot for remediation
- SOC 2 and ISO 27001 evidence artefacts generated from engagement
