## Scope

Design the phishing simulation and security awareness programme. Create the campaign library, measurement framework, and quarterly delivery schedule.

## Deliverables

1. Phishing simulation campaign library: ≥ 5 campaign templates covering different TTPs
2. Landing page: educational redirect page for staff who click simulated phishing
3. Measurement dashboard: click rate, report rate, credential submission rate per campaign
4. Quarterly campaign schedule and calendar
5. Security awareness training modules: phishing, password hygiene, device security, social engineering
6. Training completion tracking integrated with compliance evidence for SOC 2

## Success Criteria

- Campaign library approved by ChiefGuard for realistic but non-harmful content
- First phishing simulation campaign deployed to all staff
- Measurement dashboard operational and visible to CEO
- Training completion rate ≥ 90% of staff within first quarter
- Compliance evidence of training linked to SOC 2 control CC6.7
