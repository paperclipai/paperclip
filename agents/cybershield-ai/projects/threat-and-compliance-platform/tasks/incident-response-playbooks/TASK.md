## Scope

Create and test runbooks for all major incident types. Each playbook must be executable by IRBot with clear decision points for CEO escalation.

## Deliverables

1. Playbook: Credential Compromise (account takeover, phishing-derived access)
2. Playbook: Ransomware Detection and Containment
3. Playbook: Data Exfiltration Response
4. Playbook: DDoS Mitigation
5. Playbook: Phishing Campaign Response
6. Playbook: Unauthorised Access (insider and external)
7. Escalation matrix: which incidents reach CEO and under what conditions

## Success Criteria

- All 6 playbooks reviewed and approved by ChiefGuard (CISO)
- Each playbook includes: trigger conditions, step-by-step actions, evidence preservation steps, escalation thresholds
- Escalation matrix documented and agreed with ShieldCEO
- Playbooks stored in Paperclip as task templates for recurring use
