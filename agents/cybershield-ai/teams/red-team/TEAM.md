The Red Team function provides adversarial validation of the company's defences. RedAgent runs scheduled penetration tests and re-validates remediations, while AwareBot tests the human layer through phishing simulations and security awareness training. Both agents report directly to the CEO, ensuring their findings are independent of the CISO's defensive operations and reach the board without filtering.
