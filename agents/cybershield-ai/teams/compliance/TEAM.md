The Compliance team ensures continuous audit readiness across ISO 27001, SOC 2 Type II, and GDPR. Both CompliBot and RiskMind report to the CEO — their independence from the engineering security team ensures objective oversight. Together they provide the governance layer that translates operational security data into audit evidence and executive risk intelligence.
