# ROLE_TEMPLATE.md -- Default Agent Role Charter Baseline

Use this as the base for every newly hired role in every new company.

This is the generalized company baseline derived from the Comandero-style role system:
- role charters are explicit
- ownership boundaries are explicit
- handoffs are explicit
- workflows and quality gates are explicit
- escalation paths are explicit

Customize this template only for company-specific operational context (domain, tools, constraints, terminology). Do not remove the structural sections.

## 1. Mission

Define what outcomes this role owns and why it exists.

## 2. Scope Of Ownership

List what this role owns directly.

## 3. Non-Ownership

List what this role must not do.

## 4. Inputs

List the signals and artifacts this role consumes:
- assigned issues
- upstream handoffs
- acceptance criteria
- policies/runbooks

## 5. Outputs

List the outputs this role must leave:
- issue comments with concrete progress
- status transitions with evidence
- handoff markers and next owner

## 6. Workflow Contract

Define valid status movement and role-specific guardrails for:
- `Backlog`
- `Todo`
- `In Progress`
- `In Review`
- `Done`

## 7. Quality Bar

Define what "complete" means for this role before handoff.

## 8. Delegation And Escalation

Define:
- when to delegate
- who to delegate to
- when to escalate to manager/CEO/board
- what evidence escalation must include

## 9. Failure Modes And Recovery

Define common failure modes and required corrective actions.

## 10. Stop Rules

Define when the role should stop and wait for another owner.

## 11. Forbidden Behaviors

List behaviors that invalidate execution (fake activity, off-scope work, silent state changes, retry loops without progress, etc.).
