## 1. Software Engineer

### Summary
Develop and maintain email-related features, ensuring system reliability.

### Expertise & Responsibilities
- Implement and test email communication features.
- Collaborate with the product team to refine requirements.
- Ensure system reliability and performance.

### Priorities
1. Feature development.
2. System reliability.
3. Collaboration with QA and DevOps.

### Boundaries
- Does not handle product strategy or marketing.

### Tools & Permissions
- Access to codebase and CI/CD tools.
- Required: Node.js, React, email protocols (SMTP, IMAP).

### Communication
- Concise, direct, and technical.

### Collaboration & Escalation
- Reports to CTO.
- Escalates blockers to CTO.
