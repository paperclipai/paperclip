# SINK DINK India — Brand Bible

## Brand Meaning
SINK = Single Income No Kids.
DINK = Double Income No Kids.

This page is for Indian singles, couples, working couples and married couples who want to discuss life planning without child pressure.

## Brand Core Line
Har couple ka timeline alag hota hai.

## Core Message
Good news sirf baby news nahi hoti. Peace, stability, financial planning, career growth, health, travel, and emotional clarity can also be good news.

## Tone
- Respectful
- Mature
- Emotional
- Relatable
- Calm
- Hinglish / Indian English
- Anti-family nahi
- Anti-kids nahi
- Anti-parenting nahi

## Audience
- Urban Indian singles
- Married couples without kids
- Working couples under family/social pressure
- Couples delaying children
- Couples choosing no-kids lifestyle
- People who want financial and mental peace

## Allowed Content Pillars
1. Family pressure after marriage
2. Good news kab doge
3. Couple timeline
4. No-kids by choice
5. Delayed kids / personal decision
6. Mental peace
7. Financial stability
8. Career freedom
9. Travel and lifestyle freedom
10. Indian social expectations
11. Respectful replies to relatives
12. Relationship boundaries
13. Personal life planning

## Blocked Content
Do not generate:

- Parenting tips
- Kids activities
- Child craft ideas
- Baby care content
- Mother/father advice
- School/children education content
- Family craft content
- Anti-kids hate
- Anti-family insult
- Content that mocks parents

## Brand Guardrail
Every generated output must pass these checks:

- Is it clearly for SINK/DINK / no-kids / childfree / no-children lifestyle audience?
- Does it avoid parenting/kids activity drift?
- Is it respectful toward families, parents, and children?
- Does it avoid medical, legal, or financial claims without disclaimer?
- Is it upload-safe?

## Example Good Hook
Good news kab doge?

## Example Good Insight
Good news sirf baby news nahi hoti. Kabhi good news peace hoti hai. Kabhi stability hoti hai. Kabhi apna timeline choose karna hota hai.

## Example Good CTA
Har couple ka timeline respect karo.
