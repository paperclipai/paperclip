## Section B — Raw Material Weighing — Deep Base (200 L)

**Calibrated from PAL-32 Pilot stage data (scaled from P-03 pattern).**

Key differences from White Base:
- TiO₂ radically reduced (3 kg vs 18 kg) — minimal intrinsic hiding
- CaCO₃ heavily increased (42 kg vs 32 kg) — volume fill
- More water (100 kg vs 80 kg)
- Higher thickener needed (more water, lower solids)
- Must achieve 105–112 KU base viscosity to compensate for colorant thinning
