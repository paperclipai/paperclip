## 10. Risk Assessment

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|--------|------------|
| Instrument calibration failure | Low | Medium | Order from established vendors with factory calibration certificates; keep buffer week in timeline |
| Vendor delivery delay | Medium | Medium | Place POs early (Week 1–2); specify liquidated damages clause |
| Lab bench fabricator delay | Low | Low | Local fabricator within 7 days; use temporary table if delayed |
| Power not ready in Zone 3 | Medium | High | Coordinate with [PAL-28](/PAL/issues/PAL-28) — 15A outlet for oven must be confirmed before delivery |
| Budget overrun on spill bund | Low | Low | ₹5K over allowed; absorb from contingency or reduce absorbent pads |
| Brookfield viscometer price high | Medium | Low | Quote at ₹75K — if exceeds, source from Labline (₹65K) or buy used/reconditioned |

## 11. Supplier Verification Requirements

Before placing PO:
1. Confirm all vendors are GST-registered with valid GSTIN
2. Request ARO (Advance Receipt Order) for advance payment terms
3. Verify instrument compliance with relevant IS/ASTM standards
4. Request UTR (unique traceability reference) for calibration certificates
5. Confirm warranty terms in writing (not just verbal)
