# Incoming Raw Material QC Specifications

**Document code:** PAL-21-QC-001
**Date:** 2026-05-28
**Author:** CTO
**Status:** Final — ready for validation

## 1. Purpose

This document defines the incoming quality control specifications, test methods, and pass/fail limits for all 12 raw materials used in paint base manufacturing. Every incoming lot must pass these checks before being accepted into inventory.

## 3. Detailed Test Methods

### 3.1 Water (DM)
- **Conductivity:** ASTM D1125 — inline conductivity meter, record µS/cm at 25°C
- **pH:** ISO 787-9 — calibrated pH meter
- **Appearance:** Visual — 500 mL clear glass beaker against white background

### 3.2 TiO₂ — Rutile
- **Whiteness (CIE L*):** ISO 2469 — spectrophotometer with D65 illuminant, 10° observer, pressed powder pellet
- **Residue on 45 µm sieve:** IS 13428 — wet sieving, weigh retained fraction after drying
- **Moisture:** IS 13428 — 2 g sample, 105°C, 2 hr, loss on drying
- **Oil absorption:** ISO 787-5 — linseed oil titration to stiff paste endpoint
- **Cr(VI) content:** US EPA 3060A/7196A — diphenylcarbazide colorimetric method; limit <10 ppm

### 3.3 CaCO₃ (Ground)
- **Fineness:** IS 17620 — wet sieving on 325 mesh (45 µm). Pass through 25 g sample. Accept ≤ 0.5% retained
- **Brightness:** ISO 2469 — same as TiO₂; measure at 457 nm (Ry)
- **Moisture:** 5 g at 105°C, 1 hr
- **pH of 10% slurry:** Stir 10 g in 100 mL DM water, measure with calibrated pH meter after 5 min

### 3.4 Talc (Micro-fine)
- **Fineness:** IS 17620 — wet sieve 325 mesh
- **Brightness:** ISO 2469 — Ry at 457 nm
- **Loose bulk density:** Weigh 100 mL graduated cylinder filled without tapping

### 3.5 Kaolin (China Clay)
- Tests as per CaCO₃ (fineness, brightness, moisture, pH of 10% slurry)
- Brightness threshold lower (≥ 82%) vs CaCO₃ (≥ 90%) due to natural colour

### 3.6 Styrene-Acrylic Emulsion
- **Solids:** ISO 3251 — 1 g sample, 105°C, 1 hr; target 50±1%
- **pH:** Direct electrode immersion; target 7.0–8.5
- **Viscosity:** Brookfield LVT, spindle #2 at 60 rpm; target <1000 cP
- **Coagulum:** Filter 100 g emulsion through 100 µm nylon mesh, wash, dry, weigh; target <0.05%
- **Particle size:** Malvern Zetasizer or equivalent; target 0.15–0.25 µm
- **MEQ (Mechanical Emulsion Stability):** Stir 100 g at 3000 rpm, 30 min, filter 100 µm; pass if weight gain <0.1 g

### 3.7 Dispersant (Polyacrylate)
- **Solids:** ISO 3251 — 1 g, 105°C, 1 hr
- **pH:** Direct immersion
- **Viscosity:** Brookfield LVT, spindle #1 at 60 rpm

### 3.8 Wetting Agent (Non-ionic)
- **Appearance & pH:** Visual + direct pH
- **HLB value:** Calculate from formulation data or turbidimetric titration
- **Cloud point:** Heat 1% solution, record temperature at which solution becomes turbid

### 3.9 Defoamer
- **Appearance:** Visual inspection for separation, sedimentation
- **Density:** ISO 2811 — density cup at 25°C
- **Viscosity:** Brookfield LVT, spindle #2 at 30 rpm
- **Emulsion stability:** 50 g in sealed jar, 50°C oven, 24 hr; no visible oil separation

### 3.10 Biocide (BIT/MIT)
- **Active content:** HPLC — external standard method; BIT+MIT ≥ 2.5% w/w
- **pH:** Direct immersion
- **Heavy metals:** ICP-OES; Pb, Cd, Cr, Hg each <1 ppm
- **Appearance:** Clear to slightly yellow liquid, no sediment

### 3.11 Texanol (Coalescent)
- **Purity:** GC-FID area%; target ≥ 98%
- **Density:** ISO 2811 — density cup at 25°C
- **Water content:** Karl Fischer titration; target <0.1%

### 3.12 HEC / HASE Thickener
- **Viscosity of 2% solution:** Weigh 2 g powder into 98 g DM water, stir 30 min at 500 rpm, measure Brookfield LVT spindle #2 at 30 rpm after 24 hr rest
- **pH of 2% solution:** Direct immersion
- **Appearance:** Free-flowing powder, no lumps, colour consistent with reference
- **Moisture:** 2 g at 105°C, 2 hr

| Water (DM) | Inline — grab sample from recirculation loop | 1 L | Clean glass bottle |

## 5. Rejected Material Handling

1. **Tag** the lot as **HOLD — QC REJECTED** with the reject tag (red, dated, signed)
2. **Isolate** in a designated rejection zone
3. **Notify** procurement within 1 working day
4. **Return** to supplier at their cost within 14 days, or negotiate downgrade/credit
5. **Record** the rejection in the Non-Conformance Register (PAL-21-QC-NCR)

---

## 6. Supplier Lot Traceability

Each incoming lot must be labelled with:
- Supplier name and batch/lot number
- Date of receipt
- QC sample ID (sequential: RM-001, RM-002...)
- CoA reference from supplier
- QC test results summary

All records filed in Incoming QC Binder (PAL-21-QC-001R).
