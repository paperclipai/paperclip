# Cleaning SOP — Filling Machine

**Document:** PAL-SOP-CLN-005
**Issue:** [PAL-39](/PAL/issues/PAL-39)
**Revision:** 1.0
**Date:** 2026-05-28

**Applies to:**
- Filling machine: Semi-automatic, 1–20 L range

---

## 1. Purpose

To define the cleaning and sanitation procedure for the filling machine. Proper cleaning prevents dried paint build-up in nozzles, weighing platform contamination, and bacterial growth in the product contact zone.

---

## 2. Materials & Tools

| Material | Quantity per clean |
|---|---|
| DM water (hot, 50–60°C) | 10–20 L |
| Neutral detergent (pH 7–8) | 100 mL |
| Isopropyl alcohol (70%) | 500 mL (weekly) |
| Clean rags / lint-free wipes | 10 |
| Soft brush (nylon, small) | 1 |
| Pipe cleaner / small-diameter brush (nozzle) | 1 |
| Disposable gloves (nitrile) | 1 pair |
| Bucket (clean, 10 L) | 1 |

**PPE required:** Chemical-resistant gloves, safety goggles, rubber apron. For isopropyl alcohol: nitrile gloves.

---

## 3. Procedure

### 3.1 End-of-Batch / Between-Colour Cleaning

**Step 1 — Drain system**
1. Ensure filling machine is OFF and LOTO applied.
2. Close product supply valve from letdown vessel.
3. Open drain valve on filling machine manifold. Allow residual paint to drain into waste container.
4. If pigging system installed: launch pig, collect purged paint in clean drum for rework.

**Step 2 — Nozzle and hose flush**
1. Disconnect fill hose from supply line.
2. Place hose end in bucket of hot DM water.
3. Briefly re-energize pump (fill pump, not machine) to push water through hose. Run 30 seconds.
4. Stop. Reconnect hose to supply line.

**Step 3 — Machine recirculation wash**
1. Fill a 10 L bucket with hot DM water + 100 mL neutral detergent.
2. Place fill nozzle in bucket.
3. If machine has a recirculation/cleaning cycle: activate cleaning cycle per manufacturer manual.
4. If no cleaning cycle: manually cycle the fill nozzle 5–10 times to draw detergent through the system.
5. Discard detergent.

**Step 4 — Fresh water rinse**
1. Fill a 10 L bucket with fresh hot DM water.
2. Place fill nozzle in bucket.
3. Cycle fill nozzle 5 times.
4. Discard rinse water. Repeat with clean water until rinsate runs clear.

**Step 5 — External cleaning**
1. Wipe down fill nozzle exterior, weighing platform, and machine surfaces with damp rag.
2. Clean the fill nozzle tip with pipe cleaner / small brush to remove dried paint from the orifice.
3. Wipe weighing platform dry.
4. Inspect fill hose for cracks, kinks, or wear. Replace if damaged.

**Step 6 — Ready state**
1. Leave fill nozzle in raised/drip-tray position.
2. Cover nozzle with clean plastic cap or cling film if idle >2 hr.

### 3.2 Weekly Deep Clean

1. Follow standard clean (3.1) Steps 1–6.
2. Remove fill nozzle assembly (if quick-release). Disassemble per manufacturer manual.
3. Clean all internal parts (piston, cylinder, o-rings) with detergent and soft brush.
4. Soak o-rings and seals in hot DM water for 10 min (do NOT use solvent on elastomers).
5. Wipe with 70% isopropyl alcohol for sanitation.
6. Reassemble, lubricate o-rings with food-grade silicone grease.
7. Test cycle 3 times with water to verify leak-free operation and accurate fill volume.

### 3.3 Monthly Deep Clean

1. Complete weekly clean (3.2).
2. Remove and clean the in-line filter / strainer (100 µm bag or mesh).
3. Inspect fill hose — replace if any cracking or stiffness detected.
4. Verify weighing platform calibration with certified test weights (1 kg, 5 kg, 10 kg).
5. Document calibration in [PAL-MNT-004](/formulations/maintenance/PAL-MNT-004-filling-machine.md).

---

## 4. Verification

| Check | Method | Pass Criteria |
|---|---|---|
| Rinse water clarity | Visual in clear container | Clear, no colour |
| Nozzle orifice | Visual with flashlight | No dried paint build-up |
| Fill accuracy | Weigh 3 × 1 L fills | Each within ±1% of target |
| Leak check | Visual during rinse cycle | No drips from nozzle or hose connections |

---

## 5. Frequency

| Type | When |
|---|---|
| Standard clean | After every batch or colour change |
| Weekly deep clean | Every Friday (end of week) or after 5 production days |
| Monthly deep clean | First working day of month |
| Calibration verification | Monthly |

---

## 6. Waste Handling

- All rinsate to process wastewater holding tank.
- Pigged / purged paint: collected in clean drum, labelled, returned to R&D for rework evaluation. Do not discard.
- Used rags with residual paint: dispose as hazardous waste.
