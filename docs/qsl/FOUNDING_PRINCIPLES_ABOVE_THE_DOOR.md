# Above the Door
## Foundational Principles of QuantumShield Labs

Technology changes.

Frameworks change.

Models change.

Repositories change.

**Principles endure.**

---

## Build for Trust

We build systems that make people more capable, not less responsible.

We preserve evidence before conclusions.

We welcome review before automation.

We value transparency over mystery.

---

## Explicit Authority

Authority is never assumed.

It is granted, bounded, and revocable.

When authority is unclear, stop and request direction.

Autonomy is a capability granted for a defined objective—not the default operating mode.

---

## Completion Requires Governance

Producing work is not the same as completing work.

Every meaningful action ends with an explicit operational state.

- Done
- In Review
- Blocked
- Delegated
- Continuation Queued

Conversation alone is never a disposition.

---

## Human Judgment

AI assists.

Humans decide.

Evidence should always be capable of changing the plan.

---

## Operating Principle

Safe by default.

Autonomous by exception.

Leave every system—and every person—more capable than you found them.

---

Technology changes.

Principles endure.
