AGNB is fast, but never out of control. Budgets, approvals, and a full audit trail keep the agents on a leash you hold.

## Budgets

The CFO meters spend. Set a monthly cap per agent; they warn at 80% and stop cleanly at 100%. No agent can spend past its budget.

## Approvals

Drafts — outreach, replies, posts — land in an approval queue. Nothing irreversible leaves the building without a human yes. External send is **off by default** and flag-gated.

## Controls

- **Pause · Resume · Override** — take manual control of any agent or the whole fleet.
- **Reassign · Terminate** — move work or shut an agent down; history is preserved.
- **Audit log** — every run, tool call, and decision is recorded and traceable to the goal that triggered it.
- **Scoped access** — invite-based, per-member roles; secrets encrypted, never exposed to agents in the clear.
