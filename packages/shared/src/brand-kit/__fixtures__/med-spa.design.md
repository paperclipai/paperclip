# Lumière Med Spa — Brand & Style

Lumière is clinical-luxe: the credibility of a medical practice with the calm of a
high-end retreat. Every touchpoint should feel reassuring, precise, and warm.

## Colors

Primary deep sage anchors the brand; warm blush and muted gold add approachable
luxury. Neutrals stay warm-grey, never cold. Reserve semantic colors for status.

## Typography

Playfair Display for headings (editorial, refined), Inter for body (clear,
modern). Keep line lengths comfortable and generous leading.

## Layout & Spacing

Generous whitespace; an 8px spacing rhythm. Rounded corners are soft but not
playful.

## Voice & Tone

Speak like a trusted expert who genuinely cares. Outcomes first, safety always,
hype never.
