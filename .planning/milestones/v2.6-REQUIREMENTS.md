## Completed Requirements

### External Connectors

- [x] **EXT-01:** 운영자는 IdP OIDC/SAML handshake를 실제 metadata, callback state, 실패 사유, audit evidence와 함께 검증할 수 있다.  
  **Outcome:** Validated in Phase 39. SSO handshake evidence, callback-state checks, failure reasons, evidence IDs, and audit details are persisted and surfaced.

- [x] **EXT-02:** 운영자는 SCIM user/group 변경을 preview에서 apply로 승격하고, 적용 결과와 rollback candidate를 activity log에서 확인할 수 있다.  
  **Outcome:** Validated in Phase 39. SCIM preview/apply stores preview fingerprints, per-candidate results, partial failure evidence, rollback candidates, and activity/readiness details.

- [x] **EXT-03:** 운영자는 trusted local Obsidian bridge/daemon을 pairing하고 vault sync health, queue, conflict, last applied evidence를 확인할 수 있다.  
  **Outcome:** Validated in Phase 40. Pairing, heartbeat, queue, last-applied state, conflict count, blocked reason, and bridge health are API/UI-visible.

### Native and Mobile Capture

- [x] **CAP-01:** 운영자는 Slack/Teams/native capture source 설치 상태와 signed inbound source identity를 검수할 수 있다.  
  **Outcome:** Validated in Phase 41. Capture source installation state, signing status, last inbound event, and source evidence are operator-visible.

- [x] **CAP-02:** 사용자는 mobile/native inbound draft를 semantic context, duplicate warning, source evidence와 함께 review queue에서 promotion할 수 있다.  
  **Outcome:** Validated in Phase 41. Inbound draft summaries carry semantic context, duplicate warning, source evidence, and promotion metadata.

- [x] **CAP-03:** 사용자는 mobile-sized knowledge search surface에서 semantic result, lexical fallback, citation target, unresolved contradiction warning을 확인할 수 있다.  
  **Outcome:** Validated in Phase 41. Knowledge search cards preserve semantic/lexical/citation/contradiction evidence on small viewports.

### Autonomy and Evals

- [x] **AUTO-01:** Jarvis는 knowledge rewrite를 직접 적용하지 않고 evidence, risk, expected diff, approval route가 포함된 proposed change로만 제출할 수 있다.  
  **Outcome:** Validated in Phase 42. Jarvis rewrite is proposal-only and has no direct apply route.

- [x] **AUTO-02:** 운영자는 provider-backed evaluation과 deterministic fallback evaluation을 같은 rubric으로 실행하고 결과 차이를 비교할 수 있다.  
  **Outcome:** Validated in Phase 42. Provider-backed and deterministic fallback eval share `Rt2JarvisRewriteEvalRubric`.

- [x] **AUTO-03:** 운영자는 Jarvis grounding, rewrite proposal, citation freshness, contradiction warning 품질을 production monitoring dashboard에서 추적할 수 있다.  
  **Outcome:** Validated in Phase 42. Monitoring includes grounding, citation freshness, contradiction warning, provider/fallback disagreement, low confidence, and proposal quality.
