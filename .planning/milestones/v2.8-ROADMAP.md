## Milestone Summary

**Key Decisions:**

- RealTycoon2 product-facing identity remains Korean-first; Paperclip-derived naming is limited to infrastructure/reference context.
- Daily Work is the primary operational surface for v2.8, with Jarvis/wiki/graph/economy signals repositioned as supporting evidence.
- A focused identity gate is sufficient for this milestone; full internal package rebrand remains out of scope.
- Broad Windows `pnpm test` timeouts are accepted debt when focused Vitest, identity gate, and `pnpm typecheck` pass.

**Issues Resolved:**

- First product viewport and navigation now present RealTycoon2 daily work instead of upstream control-plane defaults.
- Daily board cards expose owner, due date, OKR/KPI, deliverable, price/gold, quality, and support evidence in board context.
- One-Liner capture now lands in board review with source evidence, duplicate warning, and promotion/failure flow.
- Phase 53 closed verification/validation artifact gaps and aligned ROADMAP/REQUIREMENTS traceability.

**Issues Deferred:**

- Cross-company federation full apply.
- Full app-store native distribution.
- Autonomous Jarvis apply without approval.
- Provider-only eval mandate.
- Persistent draft revision route.

**Technical Debt Incurred:**

- Full `pnpm test` failed on this Windows host because `server/src/__tests__/workspace-runtime.test.ts` timed out in the `runs a configured provision command inside the derived worktree` case; 113 test files and 721 tests passed before the failure.
- Embedded Postgres route suites still require explicit host-ready opt-in on Windows.
- Full repo/package rebrand away from internal `@paperclipai/*` naming remains future scope.
